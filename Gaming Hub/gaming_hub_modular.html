Game Instructions

Learn how to play each game

üéØ Tic Tac Toe

  • Two players take turns placing X's and O's on a 3x3 grid
  • The first player to get three of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins
  • If all 9 squares are filled and no player has won, the game is a tie
  • Click on any empty cell to place your mark

‚úÇÔ∏è Rock Paper Scissors

  • Choose between Rock, Paper, or Scissors
  • Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, Paper beats Rock
  • The computer will randomly select its choice
  • Your score and the computer's score are tracked throughout the session

üêç Snake Game

  • Use arrow keys (‚Üê, ‚Üë, ‚Üí, ‚Üì) to control the snake's direction
  • Eat the red food to grow longer and increase your score
  • Avoid hitting the walls or your own body
  • The game ends when you collide with a wall or yourself

üß† Memory Match

  • Click on cards to reveal their symbols
  • Try to find matching pairs of symbols
  • You can only flip two cards at a time
  • Match all 8 pairs to win the game
  • Your moves are counted - try to complete it in as few moves as possible!

üî¢ Number Guessing

  • The computer picks a random number between 1 and 100
  • Enter your guess and click "Guess" or press Enter
  • The game will tell you if your guess is too high or too low
  • Keep guessing until you find the correct number
  • Try to guess in as few attempts as possible!

ü¶ú Flappy Bird

  • Click anywhere on the canvas to make the bird flap its wings
  • Navigate through the gaps between green pipes
  • Don't hit the pipes or the ground/ceiling
  • Each pipe you pass increases your score by 1
  • The game gets progressively harder as you play
